Recipe: oreo shake
========================================

Here is a recipe for an Oreo Shake:

**OREO SHAKE RECIPE**

**INGREDIENTS:**

- 2 cups vanilla ice cream
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup crushed Oreos (about 12-15 cookies)
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

**INSTRUCTIONS:**

1. In a blender, combine the ice cream, milk, and crushed Oreos.
2. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy, stopping to scrape down the sides of the blender as needed.
3. Add the melted butter and vanilla extract, and blend until well combined.
4. Taste and adjust the sweetness or Oreo flavor to your liking.

**TIME:**

* Prep time: 5 minutes
* Cook time: none (since it's a shake!)
* Total time: 5 minutes

**TIPS:**

* For an extra creamy shake, add 1/4 cup of heavy cream or half-and-half.
* If you want a stronger Oreo flavor, use 3/4 cup of crushed Oreos and adjust the amount of milk to your taste.
