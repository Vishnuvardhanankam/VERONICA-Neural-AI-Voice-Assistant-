Recipe: oreo sha
========================================

Here's a recipe for Oreo Shake:

**OREO SHAKE RECIPE**

**INGREDIENTS:**

* 2 cups vanilla ice cream
* 1 cup milk
* 1/2 cup crushed Oreos (about 12-15 cookies)
* 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
* Ice cubes (as needed)

**INSTRUCTIONS:**

1. In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream, milk, and melted butter. Blend until smooth.
2. Add the crushed Oreos to the blender and blend until well combined and the Oreos are fully incorporated into the mixture.
3. Add the vanilla extract to the blender and blend for another second or two, just to distribute the flavor evenly.
4. Taste the shake and add more milk if you prefer a thinner consistency, or more ice cream if you prefer a thicker consistency.

**TIME:** 5 minutes (including blending time)

**TIPS:**

* To get the perfect Oreo crunch in your shake, be sure to crush the cookies into small pieces before adding them to the blender. This will help distribute the cookie bits evenly throughout the shake.

Enjoy your delicious Oreo Shake!
